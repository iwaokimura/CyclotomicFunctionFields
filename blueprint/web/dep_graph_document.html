Definition 2Frobenius power polynomial

For \(n \geq 0\), the \(n\)-th Frobenius power polynomial is

\[ \tau ^n := x^{q^n} \; \in \; \mathbb {F}_q[x]. \]

In Lean this is frobeniusPower n, defined as Polynomial.monomial (q^n) 1.

Definition 1Additive polynomial

A polynomial \(P \in \mathbb {F}_q[x]\) is additive if

\[ P(x + y) = P(x) + P(y) \]

for all \(x, y\) in every \(\mathbb {F}_q\)-algebra \(K\). More precisely, for every field extension \(K/\mathbb {F}_q\) and all \(x, y \in K\),

\[ \mathrm{aeval}(x, P) + \mathrm{aeval}(y, P) = \mathrm{aeval}(x+y, P). \]

Lemma 7Constant term of an additive polynomial is zero

If \(P\) is additive, then \(P(0) = 0\), i.e. the constant coefficient of \(P\) is \(0\).

LaTeX Lean
Lemma 8Non-\(q\)-power coefficients vanish

Let \(P\) be additive and let \(n \geq 1\) be a positive integer that is not a power of \(q\). Then \([x^n]P = 0\).

LaTeX Lean
Lemma 10Support contained in \(q\)-power exponents

If \(P\) is additive, then every \(n \in \operatorname{supp}(P)\) is a power of \(q\).

Proposition 3Sum of additive polynomials is additive

If \(P\) and \(Q\) are additive, then so is \(P + Q\).

LaTeX Lean
Proposition 5Composition of additive polynomials is additive

If \(P\) and \(Q\) are additive, then so is \(P \circ Q\).

LaTeX Lean
Proposition 4Scalar multiple of an additive polynomial is additive

If \(P\) is additive and \(a \in \mathbb {F}_q\), then \(a \cdot P\) is additive.

LaTeX Lean
Proposition 6Frobenius powers are additive

For every \(n \geq 0\), the polynomial \(x^{q^n}\) is additive.

LaTeX Lean
Theorem 11Structure theorem for additive polynomials

Every additive polynomial \(P \in \mathbb {F}_q[x]\) can be written uniquely as

\[ P = \sum _{i=0}^{N} a_i \, x^{q^i} \]

for some \(N \geq 0\) and coefficients \(a_i \in \mathbb {F}_q\). In other words, \(P\) is an \(\mathbb {F}_q\)-linear combination of Frobenius power polynomials.
